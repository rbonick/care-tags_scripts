Bioshock 2: I love Bioshock series. The stories have left a huge impact on my life and I'll probably get a tattoo of something from Bioshock. The first is my favorite game of all time and Infinite left a little to be desired, but nonetheless, I've played it 4 times through. I was more than pleased with the combat in 2. Was I happy I played it? You bet your sweet ass I am.

M&L: DT: I've only made it through the first two levels, but the whole game feels like a tutorial. Same with the "expert points." They force you to focus on gameplay and prompt swearing under my breath after I didn't dodge an enemy attack for the 10th time in a row. Overall, I hope the game isn't a giant tutorial because I just can't get into it like I did with Superstar Saga.
